## The consistent 4-step flow (updated June 30, 2026)

Every milestone follows the Learn, See, Do, Extend model. This replaces the prior Exercise/Model/Videos/Diagnosis order based on feedback from the Senteo consultant call: the original structure had no practice rep before owners touched their own data, which caused freezing and errors. The Do step is the fix.

Each video is 8 to 15 minutes maximum. Nothing longer. If a lesson runs past 15 minutes it gets split into two videos.

1. **Learn** — Mark (or specialist) teaches the theory or methodology. One focused video, 8 to 12 minutes.
2. **See** — A case study or real example showing the method in action, told as a story. One video, 8 to 12 minutes.
3. **Do** — A guided worksheet exercise on a fictional sample business (not the owner's own). Owners practice the method with no risk before touching their own data. 15 to 20 minutes.
4. **Extend** — Owner applies the method to their own business using the milestone tool. The model scores their input and outputs a specific, named recommendation. 20 to 30 minutes.

**Important: do not film any videos before running Milestones 1, 2, and 3 live on Zoom with 3 to 4 beta clients.** Refine based on that feedback, then film. Filming ahead of feedback is how you end up reshooting for months.

## Milestone 1: Reclaim the Owner's Time and Identity

- **Exercise:** Liberation Audit (1-week time log, 4 categories) and Constraint Diagnosis (4 questions).
- **Model:** **already built and live** at `apps/reclaim-protocol/`. Scores the time log, ranks delegate / automate / eliminate candidates by hours and readiness, outputs the 15-Hour Reclaim Protocol as a named list with deadlines (this week / 2 weeks / 30 days).
- **Videos:** The Liberation Audit Method, The 15-Hour Reclaim Protocol, The CEO Identity Shift, The Constraint Diagnosis.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, hours per week reclaimed and capacity freed for Milestone 2 onward. If not, the same 60-hour week compounds, no decisions transfer, and the business stays capped at the owner's bandwidth.

## Milestone 2: Define the Destination with Precision

- **Exercise:** Strategy Razor draft, Core Customer Decree, Unfair Advantage list, raw list of everything planned for the next 12 months.
- **Model:** **already built and live** at `apps/impact-map/`. Applies the Strategy Razor to the initiative list, outputs the filtered 12-Month Impact Map (3-5 priorities) and a focus score (percentage of planned work cut), with reasoning for every cut.
- **Videos:** The Strategy Razor, The Core Customer Decree, The Unfair Advantage Identification, The 12-Month Impact Map.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, resources concentrate on the highest-impact priorities. If not, effort stays spread thin with no real filter on what gets chased.

## Milestone 3: Install the Dashboard That Tells the Truth

- **Exercise:** current metrics tracked, plus raw cash, sales, delivery, customer, and team data.
- **Model:** **already built and live** at `apps/dashboard-selector/`. Selects the five right metrics by category, sets red / yellow / green thresholds against the owner's own targets, flags up to two leading indicators.
- **Videos:** The Five Metric Framework, The Early Warning System, The Monday Morning Dashboard, The 15-Minute Review Rhythm.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, problems get caught weeks earlier and weekly review time drops to 15 minutes. If not, blind spots persist and decisions stay reactive.

## Milestone 4: Discover the Hidden Profit

- **Exercise:** P&L upload plus a customer / service breakdown sheet.
- **Model:** **already built and live** at `apps/profit-discovery-audit/`. Scores pricing gaps, cost inefficiencies, customer profitability, service mix, and revenue leakage.
- **Videos:** The Financial Fluency Method, The Profit Discovery Audit, The Margin Expansion Levers, The Pricing Confidence Framework.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, a specific dollar figure found. If not, leakage and underpricing continue silently, the same number quietly lost every month.

## Milestone 5: Build Cash Confidence

- **Exercise:** AR / AP data, recurring expense list, payment timing.
- **Model:** **already built and live** at `apps/cash-confidence/`. 13-week rolling cash forecast generator plus the five-question Financial Decision Filter scored against current spending, with an adjusted forecast showing the cash impact of cutting flagged expenses. An optional fourth check, receivables timing, names exactly how much cash is sitting overdue beyond the client's own payment terms, usually the bigger lever than any expense cut.
- **Videos:** The Cash Conversion Accelerator, The 13-Week Cash Forecast, The Financial Decision Filter.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, full 13-week cash visibility and a specific dollar figure freed by the accelerator levers. If not, cash surprises continue and decisions get made blind.

## Milestone 6: Break the Binding Constraint

- **Exercise:** Constraint Identification worksheet, three core processes mapped.
- **Model:** **already built and live** at `apps/bottleneck-breakthrough/`. Ranks candidate constraints by impact and frequency, names the single binding constraint, outputs the Bottleneck Breakthrough Plan and an effort-adjusted automation hit list.
- **Videos:** The Constraint Identification Method, The Bottleneck Breakthrough Protocol, The Process Capture Method, The Automation Audit.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, an estimated capacity gain from breaking the constraint plus hours saved from automation quick wins. If not, growth stays capped at the same ceiling.

## Milestone 7: Build the Team That Builds the Business

- **Exercise:** role inventory, current job descriptions, delegation map draft, team check-in habits.
- **Model:** **already built and live** at `apps/team-builder/`. Outcome-based hiring templates, a delegation-level gap score per role, and a leadership-trait scorecard ranking candidates per role.
- **Videos:** The Outcome-Based Hiring System, The Expectations and Feedback System, The Delegation Transfer Protocol, The Leadership Identification Method.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, a leadership layer that passes a two-week owner-absence test. If not, the single point of failure carries forward into any Tier 2 conversation.

| Milestone | Model | Status |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 15-Hour Reclaim scorer | **Live, beta-tested** — `apps/reclaim-protocol/` |
| 2 | Strategy Razor / Impact Map filter | **Live, beta-tested** — `apps/impact-map/` |
| 3 | Five-metric dashboard selector | **Live, beta-tested** — `apps/dashboard-selector/` |
| 4 | Profit Discovery Audit | **Live, beta-tested, math fixed** — `apps/profit-discovery-audit/` |
| 5 | 13-week cash forecast + decision filter | **Live, beta-tested, known gap** — `apps/cash-confidence/` |
| 6 | Constraint ranker + automation hit list | **Live, beta-tested** — `apps/bottleneck-breakthrough/` |
| 7 | Hiring / delegation / leadership scorer | **Live, beta-tested** — `apps/team-builder/` |

All seven milestone models are built and have now each been run end to end against two realistic hypothetical businesses (`outputs/beta-test/harbor-fleet-maintenance/` and `outputs/beta-test/bramblewood-creative/`), one low-margin (trades/repair), one high-margin (creative agency), to pressure-test whether the framework and its numbers hold up before a real cohort sees them. Real benchmarks against a real cohort are still the next-level validation, but this round caught and fixed three things that would otherwise have surfaced for the first time in front of a paying client:

1. **Profit Discovery Audit math was misleading.** The "profit found" dollar figure scaled with however many months of P&L happened to be uploaded (3 months vs. 12 months of the same business produced a 4x different number), and used one fixed 50%/20% margin benchmark for every industry, which systematically overstated "profit found" for any lower-margin trade or services business. Fixed: every revenue-scaled estimate now annualizes against a client-stated number of months, and the benchmark is chosen by rough business type (trades/repair, retail/distribution, professional services, or high-margin recurring revenue). Re-verified against both beta businesses after the fix.
2. **All 7 apps crashed on an unquoted comma in a name.** Completely ordinary client data ("Smith, Jones & Co," "Smaller retainers (combined, 5 accounts)") triggered a raw, technical pandas error shown straight to the client. Fixed in all 7 `app.py` files with a plain-English message instead.
3. **Fixed:** the Cash Confidence Decision Filter only caught discretionary spend worth cutting, with no lever for receivables timing, the dominant cash problem for most of this program's actual buyer profile. Added an optional fourth input (AR aging by customer) and a new check that names exactly how much cash is sitting overdue beyond the client's own stated terms, separate from and usually larger than whatever the Decision Filter finds. Optional, so the tool still works the same if a client skips it. Verified against Harbor Fleet's net-45 fleet accounts: correctly flagged $93,000 of $102,000 outstanding as overdue beyond terms.

Full findings and run logs: `outputs/beta-test/harbor-fleet-maintenance/findings.md`.
