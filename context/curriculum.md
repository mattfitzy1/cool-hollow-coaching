## The consistent 4-step flow

Every milestone follows the same shape: diagnose first, then teach to the specific gap, then show the stakes. This is a deliberate change from the original doc's teach-then-do order, made because owners retain a lesson far better once they have seen their own gap.

1. **Exercise** — the owner does the work. This is the "asset produced" already defined for each skill in the original doc, unchanged.
2. **Model** — takes the exercise's raw input and outputs a scored, specific recommendation. New build, one per milestone.
3. **Videos** — Mark's existing skill lessons, reframed as how to act on what the model just surfaced, not theory upfront.
4. **Diagnosis** — a real if/then: what changes in dollars, hours, or risk if implemented, versus what keeps costing the owner if it does not.

## Milestone 1: Reclaim the Owner's Time and Identity

- **Exercise:** Liberation Audit (1-week time log, 4 categories) and Constraint Diagnosis (4 questions).
- **Model:** scores the time log, ranks delegate / automate / eliminate candidates, outputs the 15-Hour Reclaim Protocol as a named list with deadlines. *Not yet built.*
- **Videos:** The Liberation Audit Method, The 15-Hour Reclaim Protocol, The CEO Identity Shift, The Constraint Diagnosis.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, hours per week reclaimed and capacity freed for Milestone 2 onward. If not, the same 60-hour week compounds, no decisions transfer, and the business stays capped at the owner's bandwidth.

## Milestone 2: Define the Destination with Precision

- **Exercise:** Strategy Razor draft, Core Customer Decree, Unfair Advantage list, raw list of everything planned for the next 12 months.
- **Model:** applies the Strategy Razor to the initiative list, outputs the filtered 12-Month Impact Map (3-5 priorities) and a focus score (percentage of planned work cut). *Not yet built.*
- **Videos:** The Strategy Razor, The Core Customer Decree, The Unfair Advantage Identification, The 12-Month Impact Map.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, resources concentrate on the highest-impact priorities. If not, effort stays spread thin with no real filter on what gets chased.

## Milestone 3: Install the Dashboard That Tells the Truth

- **Exercise:** current metrics tracked, plus raw cash, sales, delivery, customer, and team data.
- **Model:** selects the five right metrics by category, sets red / yellow / green thresholds, flags one to two leading indicators. *Not yet built.*
- **Videos:** The Five Metric Framework, The Early Warning System, The Monday Morning Dashboard, The 15-Minute Review Rhythm.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, problems get caught weeks earlier and weekly review time drops to 15 minutes. If not, blind spots persist and decisions stay reactive.

## Milestone 4: Discover the Hidden Profit

- **Exercise:** P&L upload plus a customer / service breakdown sheet.
- **Model:** **already built and live** at `apps/profit-discovery-audit/`. Scores pricing gaps, cost inefficiencies, customer profitability, service mix, and revenue leakage.
- **Videos:** The Financial Fluency Method, The Profit Discovery Audit, The Margin Expansion Levers, The Pricing Confidence Framework.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, a specific dollar figure found. If not, leakage and underpricing continue silently, the same number quietly lost every month.

## Milestone 5: Build Cash Confidence

- **Exercise:** AR / AP data, recurring expense list, payment timing.
- **Model:** 13-week rolling cash forecast generator plus the five-question Financial Decision Filter scored against current spending. *Not yet built.*
- **Videos:** The Cash Conversion Accelerator, The 13-Week Cash Forecast, The Financial Decision Filter.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, full 13-week cash visibility and a specific dollar figure freed by the accelerator levers. If not, cash surprises continue and decisions get made blind.

## Milestone 6: Break the Binding Constraint

- **Exercise:** Constraint Identification worksheet, three core processes mapped.
- **Model:** ranks candidate constraints by impact and frequency, outputs the Bottleneck Breakthrough Plan and an automation hit list. *Not yet built.*
- **Videos:** The Constraint Identification Method, The Bottleneck Breakthrough Protocol, The Process Capture Method, The Automation Audit.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, an estimated capacity gain from breaking the constraint plus hours saved from automation quick wins. If not, growth stays capped at the same ceiling.

## Milestone 7: Build the Team That Builds the Business

- **Exercise:** role inventory, current job descriptions, delegation map draft, team check-in habits.
- **Model:** outcome-based hiring templates, a delegation-level score per role, a leadership-trait scorecard for top candidates. *Not yet built.*
- **Videos:** The Outcome-Based Hiring System, The Expectations and Feedback System, The Delegation Transfer Protocol, The Leadership Identification Method.
- **Diagnosis:** If implemented, a leadership layer that passes a two-week owner-absence test. If not, the single point of failure carries forward into any Tier 2 conversation.
