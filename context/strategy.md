# Strategy: Cool Hollow Coaching

This is the plan: where we are headed, in what order, and why. It keeps every session pointed at the same dates. When the plan changes, just say so and I keep it current.

---

## The goal

- **System "waxed" by end of July** — see the checklist below.
- **Product out by year end** — Business Without You live, first cohorts the aim.
- **Long-term: $3M/year, 500 clients/year** from Cool Hollow Coaching, integrated with the Cool Hollow mastermind audience.

Everything below serves those dates. I will flag when work is drifting off the path.

---

## The worldview that runs through everything

**Business Without You.** The owner is the single point of failure, and that is the problem we solve. We lead with outcomes — **15 hours back and at least $50,000 in profit found** — never with features or jargon. "10x is easier than 2x" is the philosophy underneath the higher tier: push for structural change, not incremental tweaks.

---

## The end-of-July checklist

By end of July, Matt wants:

1. **Curriculum built.** Mostly done already; the real gap is the VSL and the marketing funnel to sell it.
2. **Automated Instagram content live.** Two posts a day on a new Instagram page, built around the pain points the coaching program solves. Hooks and scripts researched from what's working across competitor coaches and the wider business-consulting space, adapted (not copied) for Cool Hollow Coaching.
3. **Financial tools built and backtested.** The hidden-profit analyzer and any dashboards need to be bug-free and reliable before they go in front of a prospect or client.
4. **GoHighLevel CRM live**, kept separate from Cool Hollow Solutions' CRM.
5. **The whole business model ready to present to the team.** Pricing, curriculum structure, and the marketing/funnel plan all need to be in a state Matt can pitch internally — see the pitch deck below.
6. **The new Instagram page posting and generating leads**, not just posting for its own sake.

## Active decisions and open questions

- **Pricing and curriculum structure.** Still being worked out — needs to be realistic and built to actually convert, not just look good on paper.
- **The marketing funnel and organic lead generation.** This is the real blocker right now, not the curriculum.
- **Budget and timing for content production.** Filming everything live with Mark is not sustainable at the volume needed — this is why an AI clone of Mark (likeness + voice) for lesson and content generation is a live priority, not a someday idea.
- **Learning Claude / Claude Code well enough** to actually build the tools above (dashboards, the financial analyzer, automated content, CRM integration) rather than just talk about them.
- **Getting the wider team aligned** on the vision. Matt needs a **polished pitch deck** that shows the path to $3M/year and what the team needs to do to get there — this is as much an internal sales tool as the funnel is an external one.

## Build roadmap (priority order)

1. **Funnel and content engine.** New Instagram page, 2x/day posting, researched viral hooks, eventually AI-cloned Mark video to remove the filming bottleneck.
2. **The financial tools.** Hidden-profit analyzer (P&L upload, surfaces leakage and margin) and client dashboards/action plans, backtested before use.
3. **GoHighLevel CRM**, separate from Cool Hollow Solutions.
4. **Pricing and business model**, finalized and pitch-ready.
5. **The pitch deck**, to align the team behind the $3M/year vision.
6. **Longer term:** program website (off Lovable, onto Claude Code), an app, consolidated market research, Plaud-to-client summaries, cold outbound and lead scraping.

---

## How we work the plan

- **Manual-first for now.** Matt drives it: start, work, capture, save. No overnight automation in this first version beyond a quiet daily backup.
- **Cheap-first on anything that costs money.** Rough drafts and previews first, paid renders only on Matt's yes, with a spending cap.
- **Quick wins, then depth.** Get something real in front of Matt fast, then build out.
- **Keep the worlds apart.** Cool Hollow Coaching in one lane, Cool Hollow Solutions and Matt's day-to-day admin in another, a sandbox lane for future ideas, so half-formed ideas never muddy the live work.
