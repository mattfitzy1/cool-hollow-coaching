# Brand: Cool Hollow Coaching

This is the brand picture: what Cool Hollow Coaching stands for, who it is for, and how it should look and feel. The visual side is not locked yet, so this captures what we know and marks clearly what is still to be decided. When the brand develops, just tell me and I keep it current.

---

## The name

**Cool Hollow Coaching.**

The flagship program is **Business Without You**.

---

## What it stands for

Cool Hollow Coaching exists to **get owner-operators out of the way of their own business.** The owner is the single point of failure; the work is to change that, so the business can run, grow, and be worth something without the owner in every seat.

It is grounded, practical, and results-first. This is not motivational fluff or generic "business coaching." It is a clear program with a clear outcome, fronted by people who actually run an advisory and finance firm.

---

## The promise

**15 hours back, plus at least $50,000 in profit found.**

That is the headline. The profit-found framing ("$50,000 found versus a $5,000 price") is the brand's signature move: it makes the program feel like a discovery, not a cost.

---

## Who it is for

Owner-operators of companies doing **$1M to $10M a year** in revenue who are:

- stretched thin and working IN the business, not on it,
- the bottleneck everything runs through,
- successful enough to have something real, but stuck because it all depends on them.

They are practical people who respond to plain talk and proof, not hype.

---

## Tone and feel

- **Direct.** Say the thing. No filler.
- **Warm.** On their side, encouraging, human.
- **Outcome-led.** Lead with the result (time back, profit found), then the how.
- **Plain-spoken.** Business coaching that talks like a person, not a consultant.
- **Credible.** Backed by real operators and real finance. We never invent numbers or testimonials.
- **US English.** This is a US brand for a US audience.
- **No hype, no jargon, no corporate-speak. No em dashes** (a house rule across everything we build).

Full voice detail lives in `context/voice-and-tone.md`.

## Visual identity

Palette, type, and imagery style are approved. The logo is still open.

- **Logo:** not locked. A rough exploratory draft exists (compass needle mark in gold and black, "Cool Hollow Coaching" wordmark) but it has real rendering problems (broken icon geometry, wordmark clipping off the canvas) and has not been approved. Two real paths remain open: commission a distinct Coaching mark, or properly redraw a Coaching-specific variant of Cool Hollow Solutions' compass icon. Until one is approved, generated assets use type and color only, no logo placement.
- **Color palette:** approved. Black `#1A1A1A` (ink), white `#FFFFFF` (paper), gold `#C8A227` (accent), a lighter champagne gold `#E8C766` (accent 2, for subtle highlights only). A deliberate full swap from the earlier teal/sage/lime direction inherited from Cool Hollow Solutions, gold is used sparingly as the single accent, not decoration.
- **Typography:** approved. Poppins, inherited from Cool Hollow Solutions' real site font, no Coaching-specific font chosen yet.
- **Imagery style:** approved. Grounded, financial-advisory photography and illustration: real business settings (a desk, a P&L on screen, an owner mid-decision), soft natural light, minimal composition, no stock-photo gloss. Full detail and reusable prompt fragments live in `outputs/creative/cool-hollow-coaching/brand-pack.md`.

When we make anything visual before the logo is locked, we keep it clean, professional, and easy to restyle later, and we flag that it is a placeholder.

---

## How the brand shows up

- **Front door:** the program and its website (and the hidden-profit analyzer as a way in), not social media.
- **Delivery face:** Mark on camera; finance and people specialists and guest speakers per module.
- **Everywhere:** the Business Without You worldview, led by the outcome.
