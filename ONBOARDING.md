# Welcome to your AIOS

This is your Cool Hollow Coaching AIOS: your own AI workspace for your program, your content, your website, the platform you are building, and the admin around it. You talk to it in plain English and it does the work. You are not technical, and you do not need to be. Your builder will sit with you for the first session, and after that you can run it on your own.

Take it slowly. Nothing here can break anything, and every step is small. You like to write things down, so each step below is one clear move you can tick off.

---

## Step 1: Install Claude Code (about 15 minutes, with your builder)

1. Download and install the **Claude Code Desktop app** (the app, not the terminal version). Your builder will point you to the download.
2. Open it and **sign in with your own Claude account.**

That is it for step 1. You now have the app that runs your AIOS.

---

## Step 2: Get your workspace

1. You will get a **GitHub invitation by email** to your private workspace. Open it and **accept the invite.** (GitHub is just where your workspace is safely stored online. You do not need to learn it.)
2. On the workspace page, click the green **Code** button, then **Download ZIP.**
3. **Unzip** the file (double-click it). You now have a folder called `cool-hollow-aios`.
4. In the Claude Code app, **open that folder.**

You never need to understand git or coding to do this. Downloading the ZIP is the friendly way in; saving and backing up is handled for you later.

---

## Step 3: The one toggle (the most important step)

In the Claude Code app, go to **Settings, then Claude Code, then turn on "Allow bypass permissions mode."** You only do this once.

This lets you just talk to the system without clicking "approve" every single time. The safety rules are still on underneath: it cannot do anything risky, and it never publishes or emails anyone without showing you first.

> _A screenshot of this setting lives with the onboarding materials your builder has. If you cannot find the toggle, ask and they will point at it._

This step matters because the file that should switch this on automatically is quietly ignored by the Desktop app, so it has to be flipped by hand once. After that, it stays on.

---

## Step 4: Say hello

In the chat, type:

```
/prime
```

This is how you start every session. The first time, it greets you, explains in plain English what this is, and confirms it knows Cool Hollow Coaching and your brand voice. It also offers to help you set up.

---

## Step 5: Build your context (the interview)

Type:

```
/install context-os-v1
```

This runs a gentle interview that asks you a few questions about yourself, your program, and your business, in your own words, and writes them into the system so it truly knows you. You can also just talk it through. (A tip the interview will remind you of: add documents one at a time, never a big bulk dump.)

---

## Step 6: Connect your tools

Type:

```
/setup
```

This walks you through connecting your tools, one bundle at a time, cheapest and most useful first. For each one it explains what it is, shows you exactly where to sign up and where the key goes, and checks it works. You can skip anything, and skipping never breaks anything. You can always come back later, or run `/doctor` any time to see what is connected.

---

## You are ready

From here, your daily rhythm is tiny: `/prime` to start, talk it through to get work done, and `/commit` to save at the end. If anything ever looks wrong, just say **"stop"** or close the window. Nothing you have saved is ever lost.

There is a one-page guide, **How to talk to your AIOS**, in `reference/how-to-talk-to-your-aios.md`. Keep it handy for the first few weeks.
