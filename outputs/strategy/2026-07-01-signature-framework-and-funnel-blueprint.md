## Part 1: The Signature Framework

### Why we need a named framework

Right now Business Without You is a very good curriculum: seven milestones, twelve weeks, seven working tools. What it is not yet is a **named, ownable method** a prospect can picture in one glance and a graduate can describe to a friend in one sentence. Hormozi has the Value Equation. Breakthrough Academy has their systems language. Every premium program that commands its price has a piece of visual IP that makes the method feel engineered, not improvised. That is what justifies premium pricing: you are not buying coaching hours, you are buying a proven operating system.

### The framework: The Business Without You Method

Seven milestones, grouped into **three phases** that tell the story in the order the owner feels it:

**Phase 1: GET YOUR TIME BACK** (Weeks 1 to 3)
- Milestone 1: Reclaim the Owner's Time and Identity
- Milestone 2: Define the Destination with Precision
- The promise of this phase: 15 hours back on your calendar before we ask you for anything else. This is the time-objection killer built into the structure itself. "Too busy for a 12-week program? The first thing the program does is fix that."

**Phase 2: FIND THE MONEY** (Weeks 4 to 7)
- Milestone 3: Install the Dashboard That Tells the Truth
- Milestone 4: Discover the Hidden Profit
- Milestone 5: Build Cash Confidence
- The promise of this phase: a specific dollar figure, found in your own books by a real CFO. This is our wedge, the thing no trade coach or generalist guru offers. Cam leads two of these three weeks back to back.

**Phase 3: MAKE IT RUN WITHOUT YOU** (Weeks 8 to 12)
- Milestone 6: Break the Binding Constraint
- Milestone 7: Build the Team That Builds the Business
- The promise of this phase: the two-week vacation test. The business passes or it doesn't.

**The one-glance version for slides, the landing page, and the VSL:**

> Get your time back. Find the money. Make it run without you.
> Seven milestones. Twelve weeks. Seven working tools you keep.

### The premium logic: "Seven milestones, seven tools"

The tools are the physical proof that this is a system, not a seminar. Every milestone ends with the owner's own data running through a purpose-built model that outputs a named, specific recommendation. Nobody leaves with notes; everyone leaves with a working dashboard, a 13-week cash forecast, a reclaim protocol, a hiring system. We say this everywhere:

> "You don't graduate with a binder. You graduate with a working operating system: your dashboard, your cash forecast, your delegation map, your hiring templates. Built on your numbers, yours to keep."

This is also the retention hook (Part 2): tools create habits, and habits keep clients in our orbit.

### The name (decided July 1, 2026)

**The Business Without You Method.** Chosen by Matt over "The Liberation Path" and "The Owner's Exit Ramp." The program name is the method name: zero new branding to build, and "method" signals engineered, not improvised. All copy, the VSL, and the landing page use this name.

## Part 2: The Relationship-Centric Business Model

The goal: a client who pays $5,000 once should be worth far more than $5,000, not through upsell pressure but because the relationship genuinely keeps paying them. Five layers, in the order a client experiences them.

### Layer 1: The habit that outlives the program

The Monday Morning Dashboard is the retention engine hiding in plain sight. By week 4 every client has a five-metric dashboard and a 15-minute Monday review rhythm. That rhythm is a weekly touchpoint with OUR system, forever. Design decision that follows: the dashboard tool should be something clients keep using after week 12, with our name quietly on it. Same for the 13-week cash forecast, which needs refreshing every week to stay alive.

### Layer 2: The graduate re-audit (the comeback moment)

Ninety days after the Liberation Finale, every graduate gets a personal invitation to re-run their numbers: fresh Profit Discovery Audit, fresh dashboard thresholds, fresh reclaim scan. Framed as a checkup, not a pitch. What it actually does:

- Shows them their progress in their own numbers (the strongest testimonial generator we will ever have)
- Surfaces new leakage, which naturally opens the Tier 2 conversation for those who've grown
- Puts a real date in the calendar so "graduation" never means "goodbye"

This costs us almost nothing because the tools already exist. It is the single highest-leverage relationship move in this document.

### Layer 3: The alumni circle

Graduates keep access to a light community layer: the tool suite, a monthly open Q&A call with Mark, and each other. Not a paid tier, not a heavy build. The point is that leaving Business Without You should feel like joining something, not finishing something. Owners of $1M to $10M businesses are isolated; the peer room is worth more to them than most content. This is also where Tier 2 invitations come from, so ascension happens inside a warm room instead of a cold email.

### Layer 4: The ascension path (Tier 1 → Tier 2)

Tier 2 at $22K/yr stays **invitation-only for graduates who got a result.** The invitation logic writes itself: "The audit found $X. Milestone work captured part of it. Tier 2 is where the full team helps you take the bigger swings: tax strategy, investment, 10x moves." The $50K found in Tier 1 funds Tier 2. The synthesis research flagged the 4.4x price jump from $5K to $22K; the re-audit (Layer 2) and alumni circle (Layer 3) are the bridge, so the jump happens after renewed proof, not straight off the finale high.

### Layer 5: The referral loop

The natural moment to ask is the re-audit, when a graduate is looking at their own before-and-after numbers. The ask is plain: "Who do you know who's where you were six months ago?" Consider a concrete thank-you (a free re-audit for the referrer, or a founding-member rate lock on Tier 2) rather than cash, which cheapens a peer referral. Decide later, no rush pre-launch.

### What this adds up to

Client lifetime shape: $5K program → free re-audit at day 90 → alumni orbit → $22K/yr Tier 2 for the ones who grow → referrals from everyone whose numbers moved. Revenue compounds through relationship, not volume. This is also the honest version of "relationship-centric": every layer gives the client something real before it gives us anything.

**Open items in this part (do not build copy on these until decided):**
- Guarantee terms for Tier 1: still undecided with Mark. Do not invent.
- Weekly time commitment outside live sessions: no confirmed number yet. Needed for the VSL.
- Referral thank-you mechanics: parked, decide near launch.

## Part 3: The Client Acquisition Funnel

The mechanism Matt approved June 25 (Hormozi's diagnostic-first structure), now mapped end to end with the VSL slotted in. The analyzer is the front door. Nothing here changes the approved mechanism, it completes it.

### The map

```
Instagram (2 posts/day, value only, one link in bio)
        │
        ▼
FREE DIAGNOSTIC: Hidden-Profit Analyzer
"Upload your P&L. See what's leaking."
        │  email captured before result delivered
        ▼
RESULTS PAGE: "$X is leaking out of your business"
        │  + THE VSL, right here, pattern-interrupt:
        │  "Before you read the full report, watch this."
        ▼
FIT APPLICATION (2-step form, already built in discovery.html)
"See if Business Without You is a fit. The call is not
required to get your report."
        │
        ▼
BOOKED CALL (thank-you.html → GoHighLevel booking)
        │
        ▼
TIER 1: Business Without You, $5,000, 12 weeks
        │  Monday dashboard habit + 90-day re-audit + alumni circle
        ▼
TIER 2: invitation-only, $22K/yr, for graduates with results
```

### Where the VSL lives and why

The VSL's job is ONE conversion: results page → fit application. It is not a general brand video. It plays at the exact moment the prospect is staring at a dollar figure from their own P&L, which is the highest-intent moment we will ever get. Two to four minutes, Mark on camera, plain operator backdrop (per the research: no guru polish).

### The VSL script structure (the "high-graded" version)

Eight beats, each doing one job. Every claim in it must survive our never-invent-a-number rule.

| # | Beat | What it does | Draft direction |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **The number hook** (0:00–0:15) | Meets them at the number on screen | "That number you're looking at is real. And it's probably conservative. I'm Mark, and finding money like that in businesses like yours is what my firm does for a living." |
| 2 | **The twist** (0:15–0:40) | Reframe: the leak isn't the problem | "Here's what that report can't tell you: WHY the money is leaking. In almost every business we work with, the leak has the same root cause. The owner is doing too much for anyone to watch the numbers." |
| 3 | **Credibility, fast** (0:40–1:00) | Operator proof, not credentials | Real advisory firm, ~35 owner clients, a real CFO on staff. Plain facts, no flexing. |
| 4 | **The method** (1:00–1:45) | The framework, one glance | "Get your time back. Find the money. Make it run without you. Seven milestones, twelve weeks, seven working tools you keep." Show the three phases visually. |
| 5 | **The time objection, preempted** (1:45–2:15) | Kills the #1 real objection before it forms | "If you're thinking 'I don't have time for a 12-week program,' that's exactly why milestone one exists. The first thing we do is get hours back on your calendar. Before anything else." |
| 6 | **The math** (2:15–2:35) | The signature frame | "We typically find up to $50,000. The program is $5,000. You do the math." [ONLY once the $50K claim is verified against a real client result. Until then: "The program costs $5,000. The audit alone routinely pays for it." Verify before filming.] |
| 7 | **The fit gate** (2:35–2:50) | Inverts the dynamic | "This isn't for everyone. It's for owners doing $1M to $10M who are still the single point of failure. If that's you, there's a short application below. It's not a sales form, it's how we decide together whether this fits." |
| 8 | **The soft close** (2:50–3:00) | One CTA, zero pressure | "Your full report is in your inbox either way. If you want help finding the rest, you know where the button is." |

Script gets written in Mark's actual voice using `context/mark-voice-profile.md`, not the brand voice file. Nothing films until the beat sheet is approved by Matt and the two open facts (weekly time commitment, guarantee) are confirmed with Mark.

### Funnel conversion checkpoints (what we measure once live)

Five numbers, matching our own Monday-dashboard philosophy: analyzer starts → emails captured → VSL plays → applications submitted → calls booked. If any step underperforms we fix that step, not the whole funnel.

## Part 5: The Tool and Spreadsheet Upgrade Punch List

All seven milestone apps are live and beta-tested. These upgrades take them from "works correctly" to "feels premium and feeds the relationship model." Ordered by impact.

1. **Branded PDF report export, all seven tools.** Right now results live on screen. A client should walk away from every milestone with a clean, branded PDF: their numbers, their named recommendations, the Cool Hollow mark. This is the single biggest premium-feel upgrade, and the PDFs become the artifacts clients share with their teams (and other owners). Build once in `apps/_shared/`, roll out to all seven.
2. **A client progress file that travels between tools.** Each tool currently starts cold. A lightweight saved profile (business name, revenue band, business type, prior results) that each subsequent tool loads means by milestone 5 the system visibly *knows them*. This is the relationship model expressed in software.
3. **The re-audit mode.** A "compare to last run" view in the Profit Discovery Audit and Dashboard Selector: then vs now, delta highlighted. This powers the 90-day graduate re-audit (Part 2, Layer 2) and generates the before/after numbers that become our first real, verifiable proof.
4. **Excel template polish pass.** The branded templates exist with validation and instructions. Upgrade: matching black/white/gold theme across all seven, a summary tab that mirrors what the app will output so the client knows what they're building toward, and version-stamping so we can tell which template a client used.
5. **Analyzer results-page rebuild.** The hidden-profit analyzer's result screen becomes the funnel's stage 4: sized leak number up top, the VSL embed, then the fit CTA. (Depends on the VSL existing; the layout can be built now with a placeholder.)
6. **Cash Confidence known gap.** The build-status table flags a known gap on `apps/cash-confidence/`. Re-verify what it is and close it before any real cohort touches the tool.
7. **Consistent shell across all seven.** One shared header, footer, disclosure line, and typography so a client moving from tool to tool feels one product, not seven projects.

Suggested build order: 1 → 7 → 4 (the premium-feel cluster), then 2 → 3 (the relationship cluster), then 5 (funnel-dependent), with 6 checked first since it's a correctness item.
