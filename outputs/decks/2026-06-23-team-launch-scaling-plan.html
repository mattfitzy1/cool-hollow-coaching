Why we are doing this
02 / 16

Cool Hollow Solutions is doing well, but it is full.

One on one advisory only scales so far. Every new client takes another slice of our time, and we are running out of slices. Coaching takes the same frameworks and the same team and delivers them to more owners, without costing an hour each. That is the whole idea.

Where Solutions sits
~$95k

A month, across about 45 clients. Good work, but capped at how many clients one advisor can hold.

How coaching is different
Recorded

Filmed once, it serves many owners. One filming instead of one meeting per owner.

What we reuse
Everything

Our frameworks, our finance and people expertise, and the Cool Hollow name. None of this starts from scratch.

How owners go through it

Recorded once. Owners join when they are ready.

After a short first group while we learn how to run it, owners join whenever they are ready, not on a fixed start date. That works because the teaching is recorded and the live time is open to everyone.

Recorded lessons, watched at the owner's own pace from their day one.

Monthly live calls with Cam and Hannah, open to whoever is at that point.

Mark runs the live Q&A and opens and closes every milestone.

A tool for each milestone, so owners work on their own numbers, not theory.

The next tier · outline only
12 / 16

Tier 2 comes later.

It starts once Tier 1 has owners with real results behind it. Fewer clients, higher value, and a lot less day to day support. It grows exponentially: Tier 1 graduates move up, members refer other big owners, and the community compounds on itself.

What it would look like

  • For owners doing $10M and above
  • Tax strategy, investment, and scaling
  • A community with 1 to 2 live sessions a week
  • The full team in the room: CFO, COO, people, and Mark on strategy

Starting price

$22,000 / year

Somewhere in the $15k to $25k range, as a yearly membership for that level of team access. A starting point, not a final call.
