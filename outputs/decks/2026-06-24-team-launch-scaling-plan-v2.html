Why we are doing this
02 / 19

Cool Hollow Solutions is doing well, but it is full.

One on one advisory only scales so far. Every new client takes another slice of our time, and we are running out of slices. Coaching takes the same frameworks and the same team and delivers them to more owners, without costing an hour each. That is the whole idea.

Where Solutions sits
~$95k

A month, across about 45 clients. Good work, but capped at how many clients one advisor can hold.

How coaching is different
Recorded

Filmed once, it serves many owners. One filming instead of one meeting per owner.

What we reuse
Everything

Our frameworks, our finance and people expertise, and the Cool Hollow name. None of this starts from scratch.

How we bring owners in
08 / 19

How owners find us, without paying for it.

The front door is organic. We show up where owners already are, give them something real to try, then a short call turns interest into a client. It costs time, not ad money. This is the engine behind the year one numbers.

1
Attract

Show up every day

  • A new Instagram page, two posts a day
  • Built around the pain owners feel: no time, can't step away, it all runs through them
  • Hooks drawn from what works across coaching, made ours
2
Capture

Give them something real

  • The free Profit Finder, try it before paying a cent
  • One link page with every next step in one place
  • Proof of the core promise, up front
3
Convert

Turn interest into clients

  • A short discovery call
  • The right owners step into Tier 1 at $5,000
  • Results and referrals feed the top back
This is the serious organic path behind the year one numbers: time, not ad spend. As content scales, an AI version of Mark's voice and likeness lets us produce at volume without filming every clip.

Starting price

$25,000–$35,000 / year

Somewhere in the $15k to $25k range, as a yearly membership for that level of team access. A starting point, not a final call.
