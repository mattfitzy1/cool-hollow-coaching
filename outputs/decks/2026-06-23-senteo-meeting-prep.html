Where We're Being Careful
03 / 10

The $50,000 figure, and what stands behind it.

The number is real. The proof behind it is still building.

The $50,000 figure comes from Mark's own track record, 20 years of personally running a consulting firm and finding that number or more in client after client. It has not yet been proven in this new, productized, self-guided format with a paying cohort. We're staging the claim conservatively until that proof exists, and we'd value an outside read on how we're handling that.
