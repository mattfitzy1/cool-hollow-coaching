Decisions Needed
08 / 10

Three things we need your read on.

Being straight about what is settled and what is still open.

Platform: Skool now, own platform later, or own platform from day one? Skool gets us selling in days with built-in FOMO mechanics. Our own platform gives clean GoHighLevel separation from Cool Hollow Solutions, but costs us weeks before we can sell anything.
$50K guarantee: how far do we go on paper? The find is plausible for a $1M-$10M business, but unproven in this self-administered format until a real cohort runs it. Recommend treating it as a working guarantee, not a marketing absolute, until we have 3-5 case studies.
First cohort: discounted beta, or full price from the first sale? Full price protects the brand and the math in this deck. A discounted beta gets us proof and testimonials faster. This decision sets the tone for everything else on this page.
