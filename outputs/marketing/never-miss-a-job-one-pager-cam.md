# Never Miss a Job

*Cool Hollow Coaching | Tool concept | June 2026*

---

## Never miss a job again.

**The phone rings while you're under a sink or up a ladder. You can't answer. That customer just called the next guy.**

You're not losing jobs because you're not good. You're losing them because you can't be in two places at once. Here's what the numbers say happens to a busy trades business:

- More than half of all calls go unanswered, because you're working.
- 8 out of 10 people who hit your voicemail hang up without leaving a message.
- Most of them never call back. They call a competitor instead.
- The customer almost always hires whoever answers first.

Every one of those is a job you'll never know you lost.

---

## So we stop the leak. Here's how it works.

The second you miss a call, the customer gets a text **in your name, in your voice**, within seconds:

> *"Hey, this is you at your business. Sorry I missed you, I'm on a job. What do you need? I'll get you sorted today."*

Then it does the rest for you:

1. **It texts them back instantly**, before they can dial anyone else.
2. **It finds out what they need** and books them straight into your calendar.
3. **It hands you a booked job**, not a missed call. Name, number, problem, address, time. Done.

And if you've sent a quote that's gone quiet, it chases it for you over the next two days, the window where most quotes die.

---

## It's not a robot. That's the whole point.

Everyone's trying to sell you an AI that picks up the phone and talks. Your customers hate that. They hang up the second they realize they're not talking to a person.

This isn't that. It's a text, fast and personal, the way a real business owner would reply if his hands were free. Nobody expects a human to text back in four seconds, so it never feels fake. It just feels like you're on the ball.

---

## What do you have to do? Nothing.

You don't set anything up. You don't learn any software. You don't touch a dashboard. You answer a few quick questions once (your business name, your trade, your area), and we build the whole thing for you and run it. If you ever want to change a word of it, you tell us and we change it.

---

## You'll see exactly what it's worth.

Every week, you get one simple message: **the jobs it saved, and the money it put back in your pocket.** No guessing. You watch it pay for itself.

---

## The honest math

One job you would have lost pays for this many times over. After that, it's profit you used to throw away every single week.

> **It pays for itself the first time it saves you one job.**
