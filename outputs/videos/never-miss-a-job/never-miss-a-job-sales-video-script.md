## The sales psychology, beat by beat

This is built on a proven sales spine. Each scene below is labeled with the move it's making so you can see the craft and keep it intact if you edit.

1. **Hook / pattern interrupt** — open on their exact moment of pain, not on us.
2. **Problem + agitation** — make the invisible loss visible and quantified.
3. **Remove the blame** — "it's not your fault," which lowers defenses and builds trust.
4. **Unique mechanism** — the one thing that makes this different (a text, not a robot).
5. **Future pacing** — let them feel the result before they buy.
6. **Risk reversal** — "pays for itself in one job," so the decision feels safe.
7. **Single clear call to action** — one thing to do, no choices.
