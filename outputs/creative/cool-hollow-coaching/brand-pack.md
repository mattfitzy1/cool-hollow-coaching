# Brand Pack: Cool Hollow Coaching

**Status: draft, awaiting owner approval before `/creative` generates anything.**

Built from `context/brand.md`, `context/voice-and-tone.md`, and a visual read of `coolhollowsolutions.com` (the sister firm, used here as the requested starting point). No Cool Hollow Coaching website or Instagram exists yet, so this pack is constructed, not scraped, from the actual brand.

---

## 1. North star

Get owner-operators out of the way of their own business. Grounded, practical, results-first. Not motivational fluff, not generic business coaching. A program fronted by people who actually run an advisory and finance firm.

## 2. Story pillars

- **The trap:** the owner built a job, not a business. They are the single point of failure.
- **The proof:** $50,000 in profit found, hiding in plain sight, in nearly every $1M-$10M business.
- **The destination:** 15 hours back, a business that runs without the owner in every seat.
- **The credibility:** real operators, real finance, drawn from 20 years of running Cool Hollow Solutions.

## 3. Voice

Direct, warm, outcome-led, plain-spoken. US English. No hype words, no corporate jargon, no em dashes, no invented numbers or testimonials. Full detail in `context/voice-and-tone.md`.

## 4. Visual identity

A deliberate full swap from the earlier teal/sage/lime direction (which was only ever a starting point inherited from Cool Hollow Solutions) to a black, white, and gold palette:

| Token | Value | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Ink (black) | `#1A1A1A` | Primary text and dark surfaces |
| Dark panel | `#0D0D0D` | Near-black, for deepest backgrounds |
| Accent (gold) | `#C8A227` | The single accent color, used sparingly |
| Accent 2 (champagne gold) | `#E8C766` | Lighter gold, subtle highlights only |
| Paper | `#FFFFFF` | Pure white, for high-contrast readability |
| Font | Poppins | Carried over from the previous direction, still no Coaching-specific font chosen |

**Logo: decided, not yet drawn.** Coaching keeps the compass/needle device from Cool Hollow Solutions, on purpose, recolored to gold and black to reinforce the shared lineage without reusing Solutions' actual mark. The exploratory draft (`cool-hollow-coaching-logo-draft.svg`) is already the right colors and the right concept, but it has real, unfixed rendering problems, the compass icon renders as a broken shape, and the wordmark clips off the edge of the canvas. It needs a proper redraw, not a reuse, before it's an approved mark.

Until that redraw is approved, all generated assets use typography and color only, no logo placement.

## 5. Style direction

Grounded, financial-advisory photography and illustration. Real business settings (a desk, a P&L on a screen, an owner mid-decision), soft natural light, minimal composition. Reads credible and serious, closer to a finance/advisory firm than a lifestyle coach. Negative space reserved for text overlay on every scene.

## 6. What to avoid

- No generic stock-photo gloss, no fake-smiling stock photography
- No corporate clip-art or generic "business coach" visual cliches
- No hype words or vague promises in any generated copy (see `context/voice-and-tone.md` for the full banned list)
- No logo or wordmark generation, ever
- No em dashes in any caption or copy

## 7. Prompt scaffolding

`[Real business scene] + [black, white, gold color grade] + [soft natural light] + [negative space reserved for headline] + [no text in the image itself]`

Example: "A business owner reviewing a printed P&L at a wooden desk, morning light through a window, high-contrast black and white tones, a single gold accent in a pen or notebook detail, calm and focused mood, negative space on the left third for a headline, no on-image text."

## 8. Reusable fragments

- Color grade: "black (#1A1A1A) and white, with a single gold (#C8A227) accent, used sparingly"
- Mood: "grounded, credible, financial-advisory, never glossy"
- Light: "soft natural light, no harsh studio flash"

## 9. Aspect ratios

- Instagram feed/carousel: 4:5
- Reels/stories: 9:16
- Cinematic film: 16:9
