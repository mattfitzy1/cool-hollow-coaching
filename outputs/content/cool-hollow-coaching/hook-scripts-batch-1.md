# Hook Scripts: Batch 1

12 scripts, 2 per pain lane, written for Mark to deliver to camera. Each follows: cold-open hook (no intro), diagnosis, one CTA. 15-45 seconds spoken.

These are drafts. Read them out loud before shooting, cut anything that doesn't sound like a real person talking across a table.

---

## Lane 1: Time and Identity

### Script 1.1: "The job you built"
**Hook:** You didn't build a business. You built yourself a really demanding job.

**Body:** If the business stops the second you stop answering your phone, that's not a business, that's a job with better branding. A real business runs whether you're in the room or not. Most owners doing a million to ten million a year never actually checked which one they have.

**CTA:** I built a free tool that shows you exactly where your time's actually going and what it's costing you. Link in bio.

---

### Script 1.2: "Three years"
**Hook:** When's the last time you took a real vacation? Not a laptop-by-the-pool vacation. A real one.

**Body:** If you can't answer that fast, here's why: every decision in your business still runs through you. Every quote, every hire, every problem. That's not dedication, that's a single point of failure with a payroll attached. The owners who fix this don't work less because they care less. They work less because they finally wrote down what only they should be doing, and handed off everything else.

**CTA:** That list takes about a week to build. I'll show you how, link in bio.

## Lane 2: Flying Blind

### Script 2.1: "Three weeks late"
**Hook:** By the time you notice a problem in your business, it's already three weeks old.

**Body:** Most owners doing a million to ten million a year are running on a gut feeling and a bank balance. No real dashboard, no early warning. So the first time you hear about a problem, it's already cost you money. Five numbers, checked for fifteen minutes every Monday, fixes this. Not fifty numbers. Five.

**CTA:** I'll show you which five matter for your business. Link in bio.

---

### Script 2.2: "Ask yourself this"
**Hook:** Quick question. What was your gross margin last month? If you had to guess, you already know the problem.

**Body:** Not knowing isn't a character flaw, it's just what happens when you're the one doing the selling, the hiring, and the fire-fighting, all at once. But you can't fix what you can't see, and "I think we're doing okay" is not a number.

**CTA:** I built a free tool that gives you a real number in five minutes. Link in bio.

## Lane 3: Hidden Profit

### Script 3.1: "Found, not made"
**Hook:** I don't help businesses make more money. I help them find money that's already theirs.

**Body:** Every business doing a million to ten million a year has profit sitting inside it right now. Underpriced jobs. Customers that cost more than they're worth. A few subscriptions nobody's looked at in two years. It's never one big thing, it's five or six small ones that add up to real money. We typically find at least fifty thousand dollars. Most owners had no idea it was there.

**CTA:** Upload your numbers to my free tool, it'll show you where to look. Link in bio.

---

### Script 3.2: "The math"
**Hook:** If I told you I could find fifty thousand dollars in your business in the next twenty minutes, would you give me twenty minutes?

**Body:** That's not a sales pitch, that's the actual offer. Most businesses this size are leaking money in places the owner has never measured: pricing that hasn't moved in years, a customer that's quietly unprofitable, costs that crept up without anyone noticing. You don't need to guess. You need to look.

**CTA:** The free version of that look takes five minutes. Link in bio.

### Script 3.3: "One client"
**Hook:** One of your customers is making you money. Another one is costing you money. And you probably can't tell me which is which.

**Body:** Revenue isn't profit. A client that pays you forty thousand dollars a year and eats sixty hours of your team's time might be worth less than the one paying twenty thousand and asking for nothing. Most owners have never actually run this math, because nobody ever showed them how.

**CTA:** My free tool runs it for you. Link in bio.

## Lane 4: Cash Fear

### Script 4.1: "The 2am math"
**Hook:** If you've ever done payroll math at two in the morning, this one's for you.

**Body:** Cash fear isn't about how much money you make. It's about not knowing what's coming. Most owners can tell you their revenue. Almost none can tell you what their cash position looks like four weeks from now. A thirteen-week forecast, updated weekly, ends the 2am math completely. You stop guessing and start knowing.

**CTA:** I'll show you how to build one. Link in bio.

---

### Script 4.2: "The subscription you forgot"
**Hook:** You are still paying for a piece of software nobody on your team has opened in eight months. I'd put money on it.

**Body:** It's never one big leak. It's a dozen small recurring charges that made sense once, that nobody's ever gone back to question. Run every recurring expense through five honest questions and you'll find real monthly cash, usually within the hour.

**CTA:** I'll walk you through the five questions. Link in bio.

## Lane 5: The Ceiling

### Script 5.1: "Same number"
**Hook:** Your business has been stuck at almost the exact same revenue for two years. Here's why.

**Body:** It's not the market. It's not your team. It's one specific bottleneck, usually something only you can do, that caps everything downstream of it. Break that one thing and the ceiling moves. Most owners are working hard on five problems when there's really only one that matters.

**CTA:** I'll show you how to find your actual constraint, not just the loudest one. Link in bio.

---

### Script 5.2: "Busy is not growth"
**Hook:** Busy and growing are not the same thing. Most owners confuse them for years.

**Body:** You can work sixty hours a week and still be capped, if the cap is you. The fix isn't doing more, it's finding the one process or decision that's quietly rationing how much the whole business can produce, and removing yourself from it.

**CTA:** Free tool, five minutes, link in bio.

## Lane 6: Single Point of Failure

### Script 6.1: "The two-week test"
**Hook:** If you disappeared for two weeks, no phone, no laptop, what would actually happen to your business?

**Body:** For most owners doing a million to ten million, the honest answer is: it would struggle, fast. That's not a team problem. That's a you problem, dressed up as a team problem. A business that can't survive two weeks without you can't be sold, can't be handed off, and definitely can't scale.

**CTA:** I'll show you exactly where the gaps are. Link in bio.

---

### Script 6.2: "Hiring the wrong way"
**Hook:** You're probably hiring based on a job description nobody's updated in five years.

**Body:** Most job postings list tasks. They should list outcomes. "Manages the calendar" tells a candidate nothing. "Keeps every job on schedule with a 95% on-time rate" tells them exactly what winning looks like, and tells you exactly who to hire. This one change fixes more bad hires than any interview question ever will.

**CTA:** I'll send you the outcome-based template. Link in bio.
