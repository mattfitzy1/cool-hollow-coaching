# Hook Scripts: Batch 1, Revised

The same 13 scripts from `hook-scripts-batch-1.md`, now run against `context/mark-voice-profile.md`: the "right?" check-in, rhythmic role-stacking, repeating the key word instead of varying it, "I think..." or "Typically, when you're a..." openers, and trailing into the next thought instead of a hard close.

Six scripts were flagged in the pressure test (1.1, 2.2, 3.2, 5.1, 5.2, 6.2) and are rewritten below. The other seven held up and are reprinted unchanged except for the CTA, since the original batch repeated "link in bio" nine times out of thirteen, which trains an audience to skip the close. CTAs now rotate across three types: link in bio, comment a keyword, DM a keyword.

This is still a first pass on the voice. Three short clips give a real read on his rhythm and word choices, a longer call or webinar will sharpen how he opens and closes a longer thought, worth another pass once that's in.

## Lane 1: Time and Identity

### Script 1.1: "The job you built" (revised)
**Hook:** Typically, when you're a founder, you didn't actually build a business. You built yourself a job, right?

**Body:** Because if the business stops the second you stop answering your phone, that's not a business, that's a job with better branding. A real business runs whether you're in the room or not, whether you're in the room or not, that's the whole test. Most owners doing a million to ten million a year have never actually checked which one they've got.

**CTA:** I built a free tool that shows you exactly where your time's going and what it's costing you. Comment PROFIT and I'll send it over.

---

### Script 1.2: "Three years" (CTA only)
**Hook:** When's the last time you took a real vacation? Not a laptop-by-the-pool vacation. A real one.

**Body:** If you can't answer that fast, here's why: every decision in your business still runs through you. Every quote, every hire, every problem. That's not dedication, that's a single point of failure with a payroll attached. The owners who fix this don't work less because they care less. They work less because they finally wrote down what only they should be doing, and handed off everything else.

**CTA:** That list takes about a week to build. Link's in my bio.

## Lane 2: Flying Blind

### Script 2.1: "Three weeks late" (CTA only)
**Hook:** By the time you notice a problem in your business, it's already three weeks old.

**Body:** Most owners doing a million to ten million a year are running on a gut feeling and a bank balance. No real dashboard, no early warning. So the first time you hear about a problem, it's already cost you money. Five numbers, checked for fifteen minutes every Monday, fixes this. Not fifty numbers. Five.

**CTA:** Comment DASHBOARD and I'll send you which five matter for your business.

---

### Script 2.2: "Ask yourself this" (revised)
**Hook:** I think if I asked you one number right now, your gross margin last month, you couldn't tell me. Right?

**Body:** And that's not a character flaw, it's just what happens when you're the seller, the hirer, and the firefighter, all at once. But you can't fix what you can't see, and "I think we're doing okay" isn't a number.

**CTA:** DM me NUMBERS and I'll send you the free tool that gives you a real one in five minutes.

## Lane 3: Hidden Profit

### Script 3.1: "Found, not made" (CTA only)
**Hook:** I don't help businesses make more money. I help them find money that's already theirs.

**Body:** Every business doing a million to ten million a year has profit sitting inside it right now. Underpriced jobs. Customers that cost more than they're worth. A few subscriptions nobody's looked at in two years. It's never one big thing, it's five or six small ones that add up to real money. We typically find at least fifty thousand dollars. Most owners had no idea it was there.

**CTA:** Upload your numbers to my free tool, it'll show you where to look. Link's in my bio.

---

### Script 3.2: "The math" (revised)
**Hook:** I think if I told you I could find fifty thousand dollars in your business in the next twenty minutes, you'd give me the twenty minutes. Right?

**Body:** That's not a sales pitch, that's the actual offer. Most businesses this size are leaking money in places the owner's never measured: pricing that hasn't moved, a customer that's quietly unprofitable, costs that crept up and nobody noticed. You don't need to guess. You need to look.

**CTA:** Comment FIFTY and I'll send you the free tool that does the looking for you.

---

### Script 3.3: "One client" (CTA only)
**Hook:** One of your customers is making you money. Another one is costing you money. And you probably can't tell me which is which.

**Body:** Revenue isn't profit. A client that pays you forty thousand dollars a year and eats sixty hours of your team's time might be worth less than the one paying twenty thousand and asking for nothing. Most owners have never actually run this math, because nobody ever showed them how.

**CTA:** DM me CLIENT and I'll run it for you.

## Lane 4: Cash Fear

### Script 4.1: "The 2am math" (CTA only)
**Hook:** If you've ever done payroll math at two in the morning, this one's for you.

**Body:** Cash fear isn't about how much money you make. It's about not knowing what's coming. Most owners can tell you their revenue. Almost none can tell you what their cash position looks like four weeks from now. A thirteen-week forecast, updated weekly, ends the 2am math completely. You stop guessing and start knowing.

**CTA:** Comment CASH and I'll show you how to build one.

---

### Script 4.2: "The subscription you forgot" (CTA only)
**Hook:** You are still paying for a piece of software nobody on your team has opened in eight months. I'd put money on it.

**Body:** It's never one big leak. It's a dozen small recurring charges that made sense once, that nobody's ever gone back to question. Run every recurring expense through five honest questions and you'll find real monthly cash, usually within the hour.

**CTA:** DM me LEAK and I'll walk you through the five questions.

## Lane 5: The Ceiling

### Script 5.1: "Same number" (revised)
**Hook:** Typically, when a business is stuck at almost the exact same revenue for two years, it's not the market, right? It's not even the team.

**Body:** It's one specific bottleneck, usually something only you can do, and it caps everything downstream of it. Break that one thing and the ceiling moves. Most owners are working hard on five problems when there's really only one that matters.

**CTA:** Comment CEILING and I'll send you how to find your actual constraint, not just the loudest one.

---

### Script 5.2: "Busy is not growth" (revised)
**Hook:** I think busy and growing get confused for years. They are not the same thing, right?

**Body:** You can work sixty hours a week and still be capped, capped because the cap is you. The fix isn't doing more, it's finding the one process, the one decision, that's quietly rationing how much the whole business can produce, and getting yourself out of it.

**CTA:** Link's in my bio, takes five minutes.

---

## Lane 6: Single Point of Failure

### Script 6.1: "The two-week test" (CTA only)
**Hook:** If you disappeared for two weeks, no phone, no laptop, what would actually happen to your business?

**Body:** For most owners doing a million to ten million, the honest answer is: it would struggle, fast. That's not a team problem. That's a you problem, dressed up as a team problem. A business that can't survive two weeks without you can't be sold, can't be handed off, and definitely can't scale.

**CTA:** Link's in my bio, I'll show you exactly where the gaps are.

---

### Script 6.2: "Hiring the wrong way" (revised)
**Hook:** I think you're probably hiring off a job description nobody's touched in five years. Right? And it's costing you good people.

**Body:** Most postings list tasks. They should list outcomes. "Manages the calendar" tells a candidate nothing. "Keeps every job on schedule, ninety-five percent on time" tells them exactly what winning looks like, and it tells you exactly who to hire. One change. Fixes more bad hires than any interview question ever will.

**CTA:** DM me HIRE and I'll send you the outcome-based template.
