# How to talk to your AIOS

A one-page guide. Keep it handy for the first few weeks. You will not need it for long.

---

- **Just ask, in normal English.** If you can describe it, it can probably do it. Do not worry about saying it the "right" way.

- **Talk it through, do not overthink it.** Long, messy, stream-of-consciousness messages are fine. It is good at turning your bursts into something tidy.

- **Nothing goes out without you.** It drafts posts and replies; you always read them and hit publish yourself.

- **It remembers you.** Start with `/prime`, end with `/commit`. If you are not sure whether you have saved, just ask "have I saved?"

- **If something says it needs setting up, that is normal.** It will install what it needs and walk you through signing up and where to paste the key. It will not break. Run `/doctor` any time to see what is connected.

- **Money tools start cheap.** Image and video tools show you cheap drafts first. They only spend real credits on a final once you say yes, and there is a monthly cap.

- **When in doubt, ask the system itself.** "How do I add a new flow to my app?" "What did we decide last week?" "Is this in my brand voice?"

- **It is hard to break, and you can stop it.** There are safety rails underneath. If something looks wrong, say "stop" or close the window. Nothing is lost that you committed. Nervous about closing the laptop? Ask "am I clear to close?"
